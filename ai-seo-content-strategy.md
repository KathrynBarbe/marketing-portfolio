# AI-Powered SEO & Content Strategy Generator

## Overview

Developed an AI-driven framework to generate SEO keyword strategies, content ideas, and optimized metadata for digital marketing campaigns. The tool leverages natural language processing to support scalable content planning and search visibility.

---

## Objective

To streamline content strategy development by combining AI-generated insights with SEO best practices, enabling faster and more effective content planning.

---

## Approach

### Data Inputs
- Target audience and customer segments
- Core product or service offering
- Seed keywords

### AI Application
- Generated keyword clusters and related search terms
- Created SEO-optimized blog titles and meta descriptions
- Identified content gaps and topic opportunities

### Strategy Development
- Structured content pillars based on search intent
- Prioritized high-impact keywords
- Recommended content formats and publishing cadence

---

## Example Output

**Input:**  
Product: Premium pet wellness brand  
Target audience: Health-conscious pet owners  

**AI-Generated Content Ideas:**
- "Best Natural Supplements for Dogs in 2025"
- "How to Improve Your Dog’s Gut Health Naturally"
- "Top Ingredients to Look for in Healthy Dog Treats"

**SEO Titles & Meta Descriptions:**
- Title: "Best Natural Dog Supplements for Optimal Health"
- Meta: "Discover the top natural supplements to support your dog’s health and wellness."
